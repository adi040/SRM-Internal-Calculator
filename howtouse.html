How to Use the Calculator?

To convert your marks to percentages for joint courses:

  1. Determine the total marks you scored in a test.
  2. Identify the weightage of the test (e.g., 10%).
  3. Convert the weightage to a decimal (e.g., 10% becomes 10/100 = 0.1).
  4. Multiply the total marks by the decimal value to get the weighted marks.
  5. Divide your scored marks by the weighted marks to get the percentage value.

For example, if 25 marks is the total in Cycle Test 1, which has a total weightage of 10%, and you scored 24 marks in Cycle Test 1:

  • 25 marks x 0.1 = 2.5 (weighted marks)
  • 24 / 2.5 = 9.6%

* Disclaimer: Marks must be converted to percentages before calculations for joint courses.
